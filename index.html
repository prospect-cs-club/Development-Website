Who we are

As one of the few clubs at Prospect High School that focuses on computer science and coding, we strive to provide our members with ample opportunities to explore the many branches of computer science (more than 30 branches!). We have meetings every Tuesday at lunch in Room 38. Click here if you don't know where Room 38 is. We hope to see you soon!

What we do

Here at the Computer Science Club, you will have the opportunity to collaborate on a variety of projects with your peers, attend student or volunteer-led workshops organized by the club officers with topics ranging from machine learning to ???, and participate in competitions (for a prize!) every meeting.
